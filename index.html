All About Zebras

Where do Zebras live?

Zebras live in the wide range of east and south Africa. They live in treeless grasslands. They're absent from deserts, rainforests and wetlands. The species' habitat is slowly shrinking and are now extinct in Burundi and Lesotho

Quick And Interesting Facts

  1. Weight: Up to 770 lbs.
  2. Size: 3.6-4.8 ft
  3. Lifespan: 20 Years
  4. Diet: Herbivorous
  5. Predators: Lions, Cheetahs, Hyenas, Leopards, Humans
